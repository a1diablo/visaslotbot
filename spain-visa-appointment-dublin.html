How Spain visa appointments work in Dublin

Spain outsources visa application processing to BLS International, which runs the Spain Visa Application Centre in Dublin at 2A Chamberlain Court, Richmond Avenue, Drumcondra.

To book an appointment, you need to:

1. Register an account on ireland.blsspainglobal.com
2. Log in and navigate to "Book New Appointment"
3. Select your visa type (Tourist, Business, etc.)
4. Choose an available appointment date from the calendar

The problem is step 4 almost never works. Slots are released unpredictably and booked within minutes.

Why BLS Spain slots are so scarce

The Spanish Embassy in Dublin handles all Schengen visa applications for Ireland through BLS International. With limited appointment slots per week and growing demand, appointment availability is extremely competitive.

BLS doesn't announce when new slots will be released. They appear at unpredictable times — sometimes early morning, sometimes during business hours — and are snapped up almost immediately by applicants who happen to be checking at that moment.

The BLS booking system also uses anti-bot measures including custom CAPTCHAs, making automated checking difficult. Manual refreshing throughout the day is exhausting and usually unsuccessful.

BLS Spain vs VFS Global

If you've used VFS Dublin for other countries (Switzerland, Austria, Netherlands), you'll notice the BLS system is quite different:

Different booking platform. BLS International uses its own website (ireland.blsspainglobal.com) rather than VFS Global. The interface and booking flow are completely separate.

Custom CAPTCHA system. BLS uses a number-grid CAPTCHA where you select tiles showing a specific number. This is different from VFS's Cloudflare Turnstile challenge.

Account required to check availability. You must register and log in before you can see appointment dates. VFS lets you browse further before requiring login.

Biometric requirement. All Spain visa applicants must appear in person at the BLS centre because biometrics need to be captured. You cannot submit applications remotely.

Tips for booking a Spain visa appointment

Create your BLS account now. Register at ireland.blsspainglobal.com and complete the verification process. Don't wait for an alert to set up your account.

Keep your documents ready. You'll need a valid passport, completed application form, passport photos, travel insurance (minimum 30,000 coverage), Irish Residence Permit (IRP), proof of accommodation, flight itinerary, and proof of financial means.

Enable Telegram notifications. Slots can appear at any time. Make sure your phone alerts you immediately when a message arrives in the alerts channel.

Book instantly. When an alert arrives, don't deliberate. Go to BLS, log in, and select the first available date. You can always try to reschedule later.

Consider the timeline. Spain Schengen visa processing typically takes 15 calendar days from application submission. Plan your appointment well ahead of your travel dates.
